Pricing Questions

Are there any setup fees?
+
No hidden setup fees. The price you see is the price you pay. One-time project builds may have a separate upfront cost discussed during your consultation.
Can I switch plans later?
+
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.
What does the 30-day guarantee cover?
+
If you're not completely satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked. We're that confident in our work.
Do you offer one-time project pricing?
+
Absolutely. Many clients prefer a one-time project model. Reach out via our Contact page and we'll provide a custom quote based on your project scope.
