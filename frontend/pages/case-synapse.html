The Problem

The Mathemaniac had strong teaching results but a near-invisible digital presence. Parents researching coaching options in Madhyamgram and Kolkata couldn't find them online, and those who did land on the old site had no clear path to enrolment. The institute needed a website that would rank on Google for high-intent keywords, communicate trust instantly, and funnel visitors directly into an enrolment process — without any back-end complexity.

❌ The Pain Points

  • No Google presence for "IIT-JEE coaching Kolkata" or "NEET coaching Madhyamgram"
  • Old site had no mobile layout, load times exceeding 8 seconds
  • No clear enrolment CTA — visitors left without taking action
  • Courses were buried in walls of text with no visual hierarchy
  • Zero structured data — Google couldn't understand the business

✅ What Catalyst Delivered

  • Full SEO audit + implementation: canonical tags, JSON-LD, meta hierarchy
  • Mobile-first build with sub-3-second load times on 4G
  • Animated 2×2 course tile grid as the primary hero element
  • Google Forms enrolment funnel — no back-end required
  • 4.8★ rating schema and social proof woven throughout the page

What We Built

🔍

Local SEO Foundation

Structured data (JSON-LD) marks up the institute as an EducationalOrganization, with Course entities for each programme — giving Google the context to display rich snippets in search results.

📱

Mobile-First Design

Built with a hamburger nav, responsive course grids, and fluid typography using the Syne + Orbitron + DM Sans font stack — pixel-perfect from 320px phones to 4K monitors.

✨

Animated Hero Section

The homepage hero features a scroll-reveal animated 2×2 course tile grid and a dual-CTA button row — combining visual appeal with conversion-focused design to direct students into enrolment.

📋

Frictionless Enrolment

A direct "Enroll" button in the nav and hero links straight to the Google Forms application — removing every possible barrier between a prospective student and their first contact with the institute.

🎨

Premium Visual Identity

The Orbitron display font, a dark futuristic colour scheme, and the branded Mathemaniac logo give the site a premium, aspirational feel that resonates with students aiming for the country's top institutions.

⚡

Performance Optimised

All assets are served statically with no CMS overhead. Images are compressed, fonts are preloaded, and the CSS is minimal — delivering consistently fast load times and high Core Web Vitals scores.

Technology Stack

The Mathemaniac website is a deliberately zero-dependency build — no React, no WordPress, no CMS. Pure craft: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, deployed as a static site for maximum speed and reliability.

HTML5 (Semantic) CSS3 (Vanilla) Vanilla JavaScript Orbitron / Syne / DM Sans JSON-LD Structured Data Google Forms Integration Open Graph / Twitter Cards Static Hosting

Key Outcomes

  • The institute now ranks for local search queries including "IIT-JEE coaching Madhyamgram" and "NEET coaching Kolkata" — driving organic student enquiries for the first time.
  • Enrolment conversion path reduced from 5+ clicks to 1: nav → Google Form, tracked through UTM parameters.
  • A 4.8-star aggregate rating is exposed to Google via schema markup, increasing click-through rates from search results.
  • Mobile usability score of 100 on Google Search Console — eliminating mobile drop-off that cost the institute 60% of its audience on the old site.
  • Full social-media preview cards (OG + Twitter) make every shared link a rich, trust-building asset on Instagram and Facebook.
