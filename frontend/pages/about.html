Our Journey

Five Years of Building the Future

From a two-person studio to a 40+ person AI powerhouse — here's how we got here.

2020

Founded with a Vision

Catalyst was born from a simple observation: businesses were drowning in repetitive tasks. Rahul and Priya founded the company with ₹5L seed investment and a bold mission to change that.

2021

First 10 Clients & Product-Market Fit

We landed our first 10 paying clients across SaaS, e-commerce, and healthcare. Average ROI for clients exceeded 300% — validating our automation-first approach.

2022

AI-Native Pivot & Team Expansion

GPT-3 changed everything. We pivoted fully to AI-native solutions, grew the team to 15 specialists, and launched our proprietary chatbot platform serving over 50 clients.

2023

Series A & International Expansion

Raised ₹4Cr Series A funding. Expanded operations to the Middle East and Southeast Asia. Delivered 80+ projects and hit a 98% client satisfaction rate for the first time.

2024

LangChain & Agent Era

Built our first generation of autonomous AI agents. Partnered with OpenAI and AWS as a certified solutions provider. Team grew to 35 and we crossed 120 delivered projects.

2025

150+ Projects & What's Next

Today we're a 40+ person team with 150+ delivered projects across 12 countries. We're building the next generation of multi-modal AI agents — and we're just getting started.
